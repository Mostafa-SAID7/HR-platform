# Go-to-Market Strategy

## GTM Overview

**Market Entry Strategy:** Blue ocean in mid-market
**Timeline:** 3-year plan to market leadership
**Target:** Mid-market (250-2,000 employees) in US, EU, APAC

---

## Phase 1: Launch & Validation (Months 1-6)

### Objectives
- Validate ICP with 20-30 customers
- Achieve product-market fit
- Generate initial case studies
- Build brand awareness in segment
- Target: 50-75 customers, $3M ARR

### Go-to-Market Tactics

**1. Direct Sales (80% of revenue)**
- Hire 2-3 sales reps (SMB/mid-market experienced)
- Sales cycle: 8-12 weeks
- CAC budget: $15k per customer
- Focus: Tech, Finance, Retail industries

**2. Strategic Partnerships (10% of revenue)**
- PEO/HR consulting relationships
- ADP/Workday implementation partners
- HR technology channels
- Target: 2-3 key partnerships

**3. Content Marketing & PR (10% of revenue)**
- Thought leadership blog (HR data trends)
- Webinars on data-driven HR
- Press releases (funding, customer wins, features)
- Industry event speaking

**4. Product Seeding**
- Free trial for 250-1,000 person companies
- "Test drive" program (30-day free)
- Freemium tier (limited, upsell to paid)

---

## Phase 2: Scaling & Expansion (Months 7-18)

### Objectives
- Dominate mid-market in US + EU
- 300-500 customers
- $15-25M ARR
- Brand recognition in segment
- Build team (Sales, Customer Success, Marketing)

### Go-to-Market Tactics

**1. Sales Expansion**
- Grow sales team to 8-10 AEs
- Hire VP Sales
- Implement SaaS sales process
- Add customer success team (4-5 CSMs)
- Target: $100k+ ACV from upsells/expansion

**2. Partner Channel**
- Reseller relationships with regional partners
- Referral partnerships with consulting firms
- Technology marketplace listings (AWS, Azure)
- Channel revenue: 20-25% of total

**3. Marketing Acceleration**
- Content hub with 30+ articles
- Case studies from 20+ customers
- Industry report (our analysis of market)
- Trade show presence (2-3 major events)
- Paid advertising (LinkedIn, Google)
- Budget: $500k-1M

**4. Customer Advocacy**
- Case studies on website
- Customer testimonials & reviews (G2, Capterra)
- User conference (first annual)
- Customer advisory board

---

## Phase 3: Leadership & Expansion (Months 19-36)

### Objectives
- Market leadership in mid-market
- 800-1,200 customers
- $50-75M ARR
- Upmarket expansion begun
- International expansion (APAC, Canada)

### Go-to-Market Tactics

**1. Enterprise Sales**
- New enterprise sales team
- Account-based marketing (ABM) for top targets
- Solutions engineering team
- Dedicated support for enterprise

**2. Product Expansion**
- Vertical solutions (Finance, Healthcare, Retail)
- Industry-specific features
- Adjacent markets (customer analytics)
- International localization

**3. Brand Leadership**
- Major industry conference keynotes
- Award nominations (G2, Capterra, etc.)
- Analyst rankings (Gartner MQ, Forrester)
- Thought leadership book or major content

**4. Potential M&A**
- Acquire complementary tools
- Accelerate market position
- Built for acquisition at $75M+ ARR

## Sales Strategy by Segment

### SMB Segment (250-500 employees)
**Channel:** Direct Sales (inside sales)
**Sales Model:** Product-led growth
**ACV:** $30k/year (Starter tier)
**Sales Cycle:** 6-8 weeks
**Focus:** Low-touch, self-service onboarding

### Mid-Market (500-2,000 employees)
**Channel:** Field sales (account executives)
**Sales Model:** Solution selling
**ACV:** $60-80k/year (Professional tier)
**Sales Cycle:** 8-12 weeks
**Focus:** Custom integrations, executive relationships

### Enterprise (2,000+ employees)
**Channel:** Enterprise sales (strategic accounts)
**Sales Model:** Enterprise/complex selling
**ACV:** $120k+/year (Enterprise tier)
**Sales Cycle:** 12-16 weeks
**Focus:** White-glove service, SLA, custom dev

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Targeting

**Primary ICP:**
- 250-1,000 employees
- $50-200M revenue
- 25%+ turnover OR acute data needs
- Tech, Finance, Retail, Manufacturing, Healthcare
- US, UK, Canada, EU markets

**Outreach Sequence:**
1. LinkedIn account-based prospecting
2. Cold email (personalized)
3. Webinar/demo invitation
4. Sales conversation
5. POC proposal
6. Implementation

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Slow Customer Acquisition
- Mitigation: Multiple sales channels, partner ecosystem
- Contingency: Increase marketing spend

### Risk 2: Competitor Price Wars
- Mitigation: Maintain value differentiation (not race to bottom)
- Contingency: Bundle value-adds

### Risk 3: Market Adoption Slower Than Expected
- Mitigation: Deepen use cases, expand features
- Contingency: Adjust pricing/packaging

### Risk 4: Key Sales Hire Doesn't Work Out
- Mitigation: Hire experienced leader, strong process
- Contingency: Backfill quickly, leverage advisory
