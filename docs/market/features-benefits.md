# Key Features & Benefits with Use Cases

## Core Platform Features

### 1. Real-Time Workforce Dashboards
**Feature:** Live dashboards updating as data arrives

**Technical Details:**
- Streaming data architecture (hourly refresh minimum, live for critical metrics)
- Pre-built dashboards: Turnover, Hiring, Engagement, Compliance, Compensation
- Mobile-responsive (native iOS/Android apps)
- Custom dashboards (no-code builder)
- Alerts and notifications (SMS, email, in-app)

**Benefits:**
- See problems before they happen
- React in hours, not days
- Make decisions 24/7 (not just office hours)
- Alert on critical metrics in real-time

**Use Cases:**

**Use Case 1: Turnover Risk Detection**
> "Sarah's Manager noticed a red alert: 'Sarah (Senior Engineer) turnover risk HIGH - 72% likelihood to leave within 90 days.' 
> - Engagement score dropped 35% last month
> - Peer salary data shows she's paid 15% below market
> - Peer retention shows similar roles leave at 2.1x rate
> 
> Manager called Sarah same day. Discovered she had job offer. Counteroffer extended + 20% raise. Sarah stays. Cost of retention: $30k. Cost of replacement: $150k. ROI: 5x in ONE decision."

**Use Case 2: Hiring Bottleneck**
> "VP Talent sees dashboard: Engineering hiring 40% slower than target.
> - Bottleneck identified: Phone screen pass rate 35% (should be 60%)
> - Root cause: New recruiter has different bar than experienced recruiter
> - Action: Pair new recruiter with mentor, align interview process
> - Result: Phone screen pass rate returns to 60% in 1 week. Hiring accelerates."

### 2. AI-Powered Recommendations
**Feature:** Machine learning models recommend specific actions

**Technical Details:**
- Retention risk scoring (churn prediction models)
- Hire success prediction (new hire retention models)
- Promotion readiness modeling
- Pay equity risk detection
- Compliance risk flags
- Attrition causal analysis (why people leave)

**Benefits:**
- Know not just "what" but "what to do"
- Prescriptive not just descriptive
- Removes guesswork from talent decisions
- Automates insight discovery

**Use Cases:**

**Use Case 3: Predictive Retention**
> "Platform identifies 25 employees at high risk of leaving within 90 days.
> - Top reason: Stagnation in role (4+ years same position)
> - Recommendation: Consider for internal mobility (promotion/transfer)
> - Action: HR reaches out to 25 employees with growth opportunities
> - Result: 18 stay (72% retention), 7 leave (vs 15 expected). Saves $900k."

**Use Case 4: Hire Success Prediction**
> "New candidate profile analyzed:
> - School: Top-tier university ✓
> - Experience: 5 years similar role ✓
> - Interview score: 8/10 ✓
> - BUT: Compensation change: +40% (high risk)
> - Previous hires with 30%+ comp bump have 60% 1-year retention
> - Recommendation: Plan higher engagement/mentoring for Year 1
> - Result: Intentional onboarding boosts retention to 90%."

**Benefits:**

**Use Cases:**
