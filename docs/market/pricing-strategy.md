## Pricing Model

- Email support

## Discounting Strategy

### When to Discount (Rarely)

**Only in These Situations:**

1. **Long-term Commitment (18-36 months)**
   - Discount: 10-15% for 3-year prepay
   - Rationale: Predictable revenue, reduces churn risk
   - Example: $60k/year × 3 = $180k, offer $153k for full prepay (15% discount)

2. **Volume/Multi-Seat Deals (10+ organizations)**
   - Discount: 10% for each additional org
   - Rationale: Expansion with existing customer
   - Example: Customer with 3 entities pays list price first, -10% on each additional

3. **Multi-Year Expansion**
   - Discount: 5% per tier when expanding tiers
   - Rationale: Upsell on existing customer
   - Example: Starter → Professional = 5% discount on Professional first year

4. **New Logo (Strategic)**
   - Discount: 20% first year only for reference-able customers
   - Rationale: Build case studies and references
   - Conditions:
     - Customer must agree to case study
     - Customer must speak at industry event
     - Customer must provide testimonial/review

### When NOT to Discount

- ✗ Price objection (educate on value instead)
- ✗ "Competitor cheaper" (reinforce differentiation)
- ✗ Budget concerns (offer lower tier instead)
- ✗ Seasonal (no discount chasing)
- ✗ Competitive pressure (maintain positioning)

## Pricing by Channel

### Direct Sales (80% of revenue)
**Pricing:** List price (no channel discount)
- Starter: $30k/year
- Professional: $60k/year
- Enterprise: $120k/year

### Partnerships/Resellers (15% of revenue)
**Pricing:** 20% partner discount
- Starter: $24k/year (partner sells at $30k, keeps $6k margin)
- Professional: $48k/year (partner sells at $60k, keeps $12k margin)
- Enterprise: $96k/year (partner sells at $120k, keeps $24k margin)

**Rationale:**
- Incentivize partner promotion
- Partner margin pays for sales effort
- Control pricing (partner can't discount below minimum)

### Community/Open Source (5% of revenue)
**Pricing:** Free tier for nonprofits/startups
- Limited features
- Community support only
- Optional "upgrade to support" path
