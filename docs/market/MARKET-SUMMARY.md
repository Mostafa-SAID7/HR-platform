# HR Analytics Platform - Market Summary

## Quick Reference Guide

### Market Opportunity
- **Global Market:** $9.2B (2024), growing 18% CAGR
- **Mid-Market (Our Target):** $63.75B TAM, growing 24% CAGR
- **Year 1-3 SOM:** $150-200M ARR potential

### Target Customer
- **Size:** 250-2,000 employees (mid-market)
- **Geography:** US, UK, Canada, EU, APAC
- **Budget:** $20-75k annually
- **Pain:** Turnover >25%, data silos, compliance pressure
- **Decision Timeline:** 8-12 weeks

### Pricing Model (3-Tier)
- **Starter:** $30k/year (250-500 people)
- **Professional:** $60k/year (500-1,500 people) ← SWEET SPOT
- **Enterprise:** $120k/year (1,500-5,000 people)

### Key Competitive Advantages
1. ✅ Mobile-first design (only platform with native mobile)
2. ✅ Real-time analytics (not day-old reports)
3. ✅ AI-powered recommendations (prescriptive, not just descriptive)
4. ✅ Affordable pricing (3-5x cheaper than incumbents)
5. ✅ Fast implementation (days-weeks vs months)
6. ✅ No-code analytics (HR teams, not technologists)
7. ✅ Multi-system integration (single source of truth)
8. ✅ Built-in compliance (DEI, pay equity, audit trails)

### Customer Value Proposition
- **ROI:** 10-20x ROI, 3-6 month payback
- **Impact:** 30% reduction in turnover, 40% faster hiring
- **Time Saved:** 90% reduction in manual reporting (60+ hours/month)
- **Risk Reduced:** Compliance automation, bias detection

### Primary Buyer Personas
1. **Chief People Officer** - Strategic transformation focus
2. **HR Analytics Manager** - Empowerment & autonomy focus
3. **VP Talent** - Hiring optimization & retention focus
4. **CFO** - ROI & cost savings focus

### Competitive Position
- **vs Visier:** 3x cheaper, mobile-first, faster implementation
- **vs Culture Amp:** Comprehensive (not engagement-only), real-time
- **vs Lattice:** Comprehensive (not performance-only), integrated
- **vs SAP/Workday:** Modern UX, affordable, fast, mid-market focused

### Go-to-Market Strategy
- **Year 1:** Validate ICP, build team, establish brand (50-75 customers, $3M ARR)
- **Year 2:** Dominate mid-market (300-500 customers, $15-25M ARR)
- **Year 3:** Market leadership (800-1,200 customers, $50-75M ARR)

### Sales & Marketing
- **Sales Model:** Direct sales (SMB inside), field sales (mid-market)
- **CAC Target:** <$15k
- **Sales Cycle:** 8-12 weeks (SMB 6-8 weeks)
- **Channels:** Direct (80%), Partners (10%), Marketing (10%)
- **Annual Marketing Budget:** $1M Year 1, $2.5M Year 2

## Financial Model Summary

### Unit Economics (Per Customer)
- **ACV (Year 1):** $50k average
- **CAC:** $15k
- **LTV (3-year):** $200k (assuming 3-year retention)
- **LTV/CAC:** 13.3x (healthy: >10x)
- **Payback Period:** 3.6 months

### Revenue Projections
- **Year 1:** $3M ARR (75 customers × $40k average ACV)
- **Year 2:** $20M ARR (350 customers × $57k average ACV)
- **Year 3:** $60M ARR (1,000 customers × $60k average ACV)

### Gross Margin
- **Target:** 75% (industry standard: 70-80%)
- **Components:**
  - SaaS infrastructure: 10% of revenue
  - Support/CS: 10% of revenue
  - General overhead: 5% of revenue

### Path to Profitability
- **Year 1:** -$2M (invest in team & marketing)
- **Year 2:** -$500k (scaling phase)
- **Year 3:** +$20M EBITDA (scaled operations)
