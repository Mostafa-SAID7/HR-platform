## Engagement Strategy

### Community Management (Response Times)
- Comments: 2-4 hours (weekdays)
- DMs: 4-6 hours (weekdays)
- Mentions: Within business day
- Crisis/negative: Within 1 hour

### Engagement Tactics
1. **Reply to all substantive comments** within 24 hours
2. **Tag relevant people** in posts (mentions drive reach)
3. **Ask questions** to prompt discussion
4. **Respond to trending HR conversations**
5. **Share user-generated content**
6. **Celebrate customer wins** publicly
7. **Participate in industry chats**
8. **Engage with competitor accounts** (retweet, comment)

### Community Moderation
- Remove spam/inappropriate content within 4 hours
- Respond to negative feedback professionally
- Don't argue with critics
- Escalate serious issues to leadership

## Paid Social Strategy

**Budget Allocation:**
- LinkedIn: 50% ($50k)
- Facebook/Instagram: 30% ($30k)
- Google Ads: 15% ($15k)
- Twitter: 5% ($5k)

### LinkedIn Ads
**Campaign Types:**
1. Awareness (brand building)
2. Consideration (webinar, content)
3. Conversion (free trial, demo request)

**Audience Targeting:**
- Job titles: CPO, VP Talent, HR Manager, CFO
- Company size: 250-2,000 employees
- Industries: All
- Experience: 5+ years in HR

**Ad Formats:**
- Single image ads
- Video ads (demo, testimonial)
- Carousel ads (feature highlights)
- Sponsored InMail (direct message)
- Document ads (whitepapers, guides)

**KPIs:**
- CPC: <$2

## Crisis Communication Plan

**Situations:**
- Negative PR/news
- Customer complaint
- Product outage
- Data breach
- Employee issue

**Response Protocol:**
1. Assess severity
2. Alert leadership
3. Develop response message
4. Post thoughtful response within 1 hour
5. Monitor feedback
6. Follow up with solution
7. Document lesson learned

**Tone:** Apologetic, solution-focused, transparent, human
