# Event & Webinar Marketing Strategy

Strategic approach to webinars, events, sponsorships, and community building.

---

## Webinar Strategy

**Goals (Year 1):**
- 12 webinars hosted
- 1,000+ attendees per webinar (average)
- 40% attendance rate
- 5-10% attendees convert to trial
- 2-3% attendees convert to customers

**Budget:** $50k/year

## Webinar Best Practices

### Pre-Webinar (2 weeks before)
- Create landing page
- Send email invitation (1 week, 3 days, 1 day before)
- Promote on social media
- Run paid ads (LinkedIn, Facebook)
- LinkedIn Event creation
- Slack community announcement

### During Webinar
- Start 1 minute early (connection check)
- Engage audience (polls, Q&A, chat)
- Consistent slides with branding
- Clear audio/video quality
- Screen share for demos
- Call-to-action (mid + end)

### Post-Webinar (Within 24 hours)
- Send recording link + slides
- Send follow-up survey
- Personalized follow-up by engagement level
- Add to content library
- Repurpose content (blog post, video clips, quotes)

## Booth & Sponsorship Strategy

### Booth Design
- Eye-catching branding (logo visible from 20 feet)
- Live product demo (iPad/laptop)
- Lead capture (iPad signup form)
- Giveaways (branded merchandise, gift cards)
- Staff: 2-3 people minimum

### Lead Capture
- Collect: Name, email, company, title
- Badge scanner (if event provides)
- Qualify: Interest level, timeline
- CRM entry: Upload same day
- Follow-up: Email within 24 hours

### Speaking Slots
- Apply early (deadline often 6 months before)
- Topics: Industry trends, use cases, best practices
- Speaker: CEO, CPO, VP Product (credibility)
- Slides: Professional, branded
- Delivery: Practice beforehand

## Event ROI Measurement

### Metrics to Track

**Attendance:**
- Registrants
- Attendees
- Attendance rate
- Geographic breakdown
- Job title breakdown

**Engagement:**
- Q&A participation
- Chat activity
- Poll responses
- Session recordings views

**Leads:**
- Leads generated
- Lead quality (engagement score)
- Contact info collected
- Demo requests

**Conversion:**
- Trial signups from event
- Sales pipeline created
- Customers from event
- Revenue attributed

**Brand:**
- Media mentions
- Social media mentions
- Impressions
- Share of voice

### ROI Calculation

**Example (Annual Webinar Series):**
```
12 webinars × 1,000 attendees = 12,000 attendees
12,000 × 5% conversion to trial = 600 trials
600 × 2% conversion to customer = 12 customers
12 customers × $60k ACV = $720k revenue
Webinar cost: $50k
Profit: $670k
ROI: 13.4x
```
