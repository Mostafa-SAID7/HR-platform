## LinkedIn Advertising

### Campaign Structure

**Awareness Campaign: "Transform HR with Data"**
- Objective: Brand awareness
- Budget: $30k/quarter
- Target: HR leaders (CPO, VP Talent, HR Director)
- Ad formats: Sponsored content + video
- Goal: Build brand among target audience

**Consideration Campaign: "Free Trial - See Results in 3 Days"**
- Objective: Lead generation
- Budget: $45k/quarter
- Target: Mid-market HR leaders
- Ad formats: Lead generation forms
- Goal: Drive trial signups

**Retargeting Campaign: "Catch Them Before They Leave"**
- Objective: Conversion
- Budget: $25k/quarter
- Target: Website visitors, demo watchers
- Ad formats: Sponsored content, InMail
- Goal: Convert warm leads

### LinkedIn Audience Targeting

**Primary Audience:**
- Job titles: CPO, VP Talent, HR Manager, HR Director, CFO, VP Operations
- Company size: 250-2,000 employees
- Industry: All (focus on tech, finance, retail, manufacturing)
- Location: US, UK, Canada, EU
- Experience: 5+ years in HR

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- Based on website visitors
- Based on lead magnets
- Based on webinar attendees
- Based on trial signers

### LinkedIn Ad Formats

**Sponsored Content (Static Image)**
- Image: Product screenshot or customer at work
- Headline: Benefit-driven (e.g., "Stop Losing Your Best People")
- Description: Problem + solution (50-80 words)
- CTA: "Learn More" or "Get Started"

**Sponsored Content (Video)**
- Format: 15-30 seconds
- Content: Product demo or customer testimonial
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical
- CTA: Link to landing page

**Lead Generation Form**
- Prefills LinkedIn data (name, email, company, title)
- Asks: 3-5 key questions (role, company size, priorities)
- CTA: "Download guide" or "Start trial"

**Sponsored InMail**
- Subject line: Personal and benefit-driven
- Body: Short message + single strong CTA
- Best for: Executive-level (VP+)

### LinkedIn Campaign Performance

**Awareness Campaign:**
- CPM: $5-10 (cost per 1,000 impressions)
- CTR: 0.5-1%
- CPC: $5-15
- Goal: Build brand (impressions matter)

**Consideration Campaign:**
- CPM: $8-12
- CTR: 1-2%
- CPC: $3-5
- CPA: $100-200 (cost per application)
- Conversion rate: 3-5%

**Retargeting Campaign:**
- CPM: $3-6
- CTR: 2-5%
- CPC: $1-3
- CPA: $50-100
- Conversion rate: 5-10%

## Google Ads

### Search Campaigns

**Campaign 1: High-Intent Keywords**
- Keywords: "HR analytics tool", "people analytics", "turnover prediction"
- Budget: $3,000/month
- Bid strategy: Target CPA ($300)
- Landing page: Product demo page
- CTR target: 2%+
- Conversion rate: 2-3%

**Campaign 2: Competitor Keywords**
- Keywords: "Visier alternative", "Culture Amp vs", "Lattice competitor"
- Budget: $2,000/month
- Bid strategy: Target CPA ($250)
- Landing page: Competitive comparison
- CTR target: 1.5%+
- Conversion rate: 3-5%

**Campaign 3: Problem Keywords**
- Keywords: "How to reduce turnover", "Reduce employee attrition", "Prevent turnover"
- Budget: $2,000/month
- Bid strategy: Target CPA ($400)
- Landing page: Blog post + CTA
- CTR target: 1%+
- Conversion rate: 1-2%

### Google Ads Best Practices

**Ad Copy Formula:**
- Headline 1: Benefit (e.g., "Reduce Turnover 25%")
- Headline 2: Feature (e.g., "AI-Powered Predictions")
- Headline 3: Social proof (e.g., "Used by 500+ Companies")
- Description: Call to action (e.g., "Start Free Trial - No Credit Card")

**Landing Pages:**
- Match ad copy exactly
- Single clear CTA
- Fast load time (< 2 seconds)
- Mobile optimized
- Minimal form fields

### Google Shopping (Product Listings)
Not applicable (not an e-commerce product)

### YouTube Ads

**Campaign: Product Demo Video**
- Format: Skippable in-stream (user can skip after 5 sec)
- Length: 15-30 seconds
- Target: HR professionals, managers
- Placements: HR-related videos, business channels
- Budget: $1,000/month
- Goal: Video views (clicks through to site)

## Facebook & Instagram Ads

### Campaign Structure

**Awareness: "Modern HR Analytics"**
- Objective: Brand awareness
- Budget: $15k/quarter
- Target: HR professionals 30-55, interested in HR tech
- Ad format: Image or carousel
- Goal: Reach & impressions

**Engagement: "See It in Action"**
- Objective: Video views
- Budget: $15k/quarter
- Target: HR professionals, tech interest
- Ad format: Video (demo, testimonial)
- Goal: Video views (90% threshold)

**Conversion: "Start Your Free Trial"**
- Objective: Conversion (trial signup)
- Budget: $10k/quarter
- Target: Website visitors + lookalikes
- Ad format: Image + conversion tracking
- Goal: Trial signups, website traffic

### Facebook Audience Targeting

**Core Audience:**
- Age: 30-55
- Location: US, UK, Canada, EU
- Interests: HR, business management, workplace, recruitment
- Behaviors: Job search, career development, business people
- Languages: English

**Lookalike Audiences (1% similarity):**
- Based on trial signers
- Based on customers
- Based on demo watchers
- Based on email subscribers

### Facebook Ad Formats

**Image Ads:**
- Single image (1.91:1 aspect ratio)
- Headline: 27 characters
- Primary text: 125 characters
- Description: 30 characters

**Carousel Ads:**
- 2-10 image cards
- Each with headline, image, link
- Best for: Showing multiple features

**Video Ads:**
- 15-30 seconds
- Fast hook (first 3 sec)
- Captions (60% watch without sound)
- Mobile optimized

**Collection Ads:**
- Multiple product listings
- Best for: Browsing experience

### Facebook Performance Targets

- CPM: $3-6
- CTR: 1-2%
- CPC: $0.50-2.00
- CPA (trial signup): $150-300
- ROAS: 2:1+

## Attribution & Tracking

### UTM Parameters
All ads must include UTM parameters:
- `utm_source`: linkedin, google, facebook
- `utm_medium`: cpc, paid_social
- `utm_campaign`: [campaign_name]
- `utm_content`: [variant_id]
- `utm_term`: [keyword]

**Example:**
```
https://website.com/trial?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=awareness_q2&utm_content=v1&utm_term=hr_analytics
```

### Conversion Tracking
- Track trial signups
- Track demo requests
- Track demo completion
- Track customer signup
- Track revenue

### Attribution Model
- First-click: Give credit to first touchpoint
- Last-click: Give credit to last touchpoint
- Multi-touch: Distribute across touchpoints
- Time-decay: More credit to recent touchpoints

## Fraud Prevention & Quality

### Click Fraud Protection
- Monitor CTR (if 5%+ on search, investigate)
- Monitor conversions from same IP (suspicious)
- Use Google Smart Bidding (ML optimization)
- Regular audit of competitor click patterns

### Quality Score (Google)
- Expected CTR: Optimize keywords & copy
- Landing page experience: Fast, mobile-friendly
- Ad relevance: Match keywords to ad copy
- Target 7+ quality score (0-10 scale)

### Invalid Traffic (Facebook)
- Disable lookalike audiences if quality drops
- Monitor cost per result trends
- Use Facebook pixel to validate conversions
- Review audience quality monthly
