**Full Logo:** Primary brand identifier
- Minimum size: 100px
- Clear space: Equal to the height of the logo
- Never rotate, skew, or distort

**Icon Logo:** For social media, favicon, app icon
- Square format
- Works at 16px and above

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Tone

**Do:**
- Use active voice: "Predict turnover" not "Turnover can be predicted"
- Be specific: "85% accuracy" not "highly accurate"
- Use "we/our" to be inclusive
- Lead with benefit, then explain how
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Include specific numbers and data
- Show empathy with customer pain

**Don't:**
- Use industry jargon without explanation
- Make claims without data
- Use passive voice
- Be overly casual
- Be overly technical
- Make vague promises
- Use corporate speak ("synergize," "leverage")

### Headline Guidelines

**Make Headlines:**
- Benefit-driven: "Save $2M on turnover"
- Specific: "Reduce turnover by 25% with AI"
- Action-oriented: "Predict turnover before it happens"
- Curiosity-generating: "The secret to reducing turnover by 30%"
- Clear: "Modern HR analytics for mid-market"

### Call-to-Action (CTA) Guidelines

**Strong CTAs:**
- "Start free trial" (specific action)
- "See it in action" (demo implies)
- "Schedule demo" (clear ask)
- "Get started today" (sense of urgency)
- "Learn how" (curiosity)

**Weak CTAs:**
- "Click here" (vague)
- "Learn more" (generic)
- "Submit" (impersonal)
- "Go" (unclear)

## Visual Communication

### Photography Style
- **Authentic:** Real people, real moments (not stock photos)
- **Diverse:** Represent all backgrounds, ages, abilities
- **Modern:** Clean, well-lit, professional
- **Human-centered:** Focus on people, emotions, expressions
- **Action:** People using product, collaborating, celebrating

### Illustrations
- **Minimalist:** Simple, clean lines
- **Warm palette:** Indigo, emerald, neutral tones
- **Relatable:** Represent common HR scenarios
- **Inclusive:** Diverse representation

### Data Visualization
- **Clear:** Easy to understand at first glance
- **Honest:** Accurate representation, not misleading
- **Branded:** Use brand colors and typography
- **Accessible:** High contrast, clear labels

## Do's & Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Use clear, simple language
✅ Show real customer results
✅ Highlight unique differentiators
✅ Tell human stories
✅ Focus on business impact
✅ Use data to support claims
✅ Be authentic and honest
✅ Show appreciation for customers

### DON'T:
❌ Use jargon or technical terms
❌ Make unsubstantiated claims
❌ Compare directly to competitors
❌ Use generic stock photos
❌ Be overly salesy
❌ Use corporate clichés
❌ Exaggerate benefits
❌ Ignore customer concerns

## Compliance & Legal

### Must Include:
- Privacy policy link (website footer)

- Data protection statement
- GDPR/CCPA compliance notice

### Avoid:
- Medical or legal claims
- Unsubstantiated performance claims
- Comparing to specific competitors (by name)
- Privacy violations in messaging
- Misleading claims about AI
