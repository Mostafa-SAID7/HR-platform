## Thought Leadership

### Executive Positioning

**CEO:** 
- Focus: Market trends, product vision, company strategy
- Channels: LinkedIn, Forbes, Harvard Business Review
- Topics: The future of HR, people analytics, AI/ML

**Chief People Officer:**
- Focus: HR best practices, employee experience, culture
- Channels: LinkedIn, SHRM, industry publications
- Topics: Data-driven HR, employee retention, engagement

**Chief Data Scientist:**
- Focus: AI/ML in HR, ethical AI, data science
- Channels: LinkedIn, Medium, research conferences
- Topics: Prediction accuracy, fairness, model explainability

### Content Types for Thought Leaders

1. **LinkedIn Articles** (2x/month)
   - Publish long-form on LinkedIn
   - 1,500-2,500 words
   - Industry insight, personal perspective

2. **External Publications** (1x/month)
   - Forbes, HBR, industry blogs
   - Paid guest posts (establish authority)
   - Topic: Industry trends, predictions

3. **Speaking Engagements** (3-5/year)
   - Conferences: SHRM Annual, HR Tech Conference
   - Webinars: Industry associations
   - Topics: Product demos, market trends

4. **Media Commentary** (As needed)
   - Respond to industry news
   - Offer expert perspective
   - Quote in media articles

5. **Interviews & Podcasts** (6-12/year)
   - HR tech podcasts
   - Business/tech podcasts
   - Target: 50k+ listener shows

## Media Outreach

### Press Contact List

**Tier 1: Must Reach (5 targets)**
- TechCrunch HR tech reporter
- Axios AI reporter
- VentureBeat HR tech reporter
- HR Dive editor
- HR.com editor

**Tier 2: Important (10 targets)**
- Forbes tech contributor
- Bloomberg HR/diversity reporter
- SHRM media contacts
- LinkedIn influencers
- HR podcasters

**Tier 3: Extended (20+ targets)**
- Regional tech reporters
- Vertical-specific journalists
- Freelance writers
- Industry bloggers

### Outreach Process

**Personalized Pitch:**
1. Research journalist (read last 5 articles)
2. Find angle relevant to their beat
3. Write personalized pitch (3-4 sentences)
4. Send via email + follow up
5. Track response rate

**Pitch Template:**
```
Subject: [Specific hook] - comment/story idea

Hi [Name],

I noticed you recently covered [recent article].

We just [released/announced/found] [news]. This connects to [reader value].

Key insight: [One compelling insight]

Would you be interested in [interview/quote/story]?

Best, [Your name]
```

### Response Timeline
- Press release: Send Tuesday-Thursday 10-11am
- First follow-up: 2 days later
- Second follow-up: 5 days later
- Stop after 2 follow-ups

## Crisis Communication Plan

### PR Crisis Situations

**Situation 1: Negative Customer Experience**
- Response: Acknowledge, apologize, action plan
- Timeline: Within 4 hours
- Channels: Blog post, email, social
- Follow-up: Weekly updates

**Situation 2: Data Breach**
- Response: Confirm facts, no speculation
- Timeline: Within 2 hours
- Channels: Email, social, press conference
- Follow-up: Transparent updates

**Situation 3: Model Bias Identified**
- Response: Acknowledge, explain mitigation
- Timeline: Within 24 hours
- Channels: Blog post, press statement
- Follow-up: Independent audit

**Situation 4: Negative Media Coverage**
- Response: Evaluate accuracy, respond to falsehoods
- Timeline: If incorrect, respond same day
- Channels: Comment to publication, social
- Strategy: Don't feed trolls
