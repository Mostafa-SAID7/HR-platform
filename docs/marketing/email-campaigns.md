## Email Campaign Sequences

### Sequence 1: Welcome Series (5 emails)
**Trigger:** Email signup
**Goal:** Educate, build relationship, set expectations

**Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)**
- Subject: "Welcome to [Company]! Here's what you need to know"
- Content: Brand story, what we do, 3 key benefits
- CTA: Check out our resource library
- Personalization: First name

**Email 2: The Problem (Day 1)**
- Subject: "[Name], are you losing $5M to turnover?"
- Content: Industry data, pain points, stats
- CTA: Take our assessment
- Personalization: Industry, company size if known

**Email 3: The Solution (Day 3)**
- Subject: "How to predict turnover before it happens"
- Content: How our platform works, 3 core features
- CTA: See product demo (2-min video)
- Social proof: Logo or quote

**Email 4: Social Proof (Day 5)**
- Subject: "Customers are saving $2M+ with [Company]"
- Content: Case study (brief), ROI stats, testimonial
- CTA: Start free trial
- Social proof: Case study, testimonial

**Email 5: Last Chance (Day 7)**
- Subject: "Your free trial is waiting..."
- Content: Recap benefits, limited offer, urgency
- CTA: Start free trial now
- Personalization: Name, company

### Sequence 2: Trial Nurture (6 emails)
**Trigger:** Free trial signup
**Goal:** Onboard, guide to value, convert to paid

**Email 1: Day 1 - Welcome & Setup**
- Subject: "Let's get you set up in 15 minutes"
- Content: How to connect your HR system
- CTA: Interactive setup guide
- Attachment: Getting started PDF

**Email 2: Day 3 - First Insights**
- Subject: "[Name], your first predictions are ready"
- Content: "Here's what our AI found about your company"
- CTA: View dashboard
- Personalization: Dashboard data if available

**Email 3: Day 5 - Feature Deep Dive**
- Subject: "See the 3 ways this feature saves time"
- Content: Feature walkthrough, benefits, use cases
- CTA: Video tutorial (5-min)
- Social proof: Customer quote

**Email 4: Day 7 - Success Story**
- Subject: "[Company Name] saved $500k with this feature"
- Content: Relevant case study
- CTA: See full case study
- Personalization: Industry/role match

**Email 5: Day 10 - Limited Offer**
- Subject: "Exclusive: 40% off first year [Name]"
- Content: Special pricing, limited time
- CTA: Upgrade now
- Urgency: Expires in 48 hours

**Email 6: Day 14 - Last Chance**
- Subject: "Your trial expires tomorrow"
- Content: Recap benefits, final offer, alternative
- CTA: Upgrade or schedule demo
- Personalization: Name, usage data

### Sequence 3: Content Nurture (Ongoing)
**Trigger:** Subscribed but not customer
**Goal:** Build trust, prove value, eventually convert

**Email 1: Industry Trend Report**
- Subject: "2026 HR Trends Report (with data)"
- Content: Industry insights, benchmarks, predictions
- CTA: Download full report
- Frequency: Quarterly

**Email 2: Customer Success Story**
- Subject: "[Company] reduced turnover by 25%"
- Content: Case study highlight, ROI details
- CTA: Read full case study
- Frequency: Every 2 weeks

**Email 3: Webinar Invitation**
- Subject: "[Name], join us for a live Q&A"
- Content: Webinar topic, speaker, agenda
- CTA: Register (link)
- Frequency: Monthly

**Email 4: Blog Digest**
- Subject: "This week's insights: [3 topics]"
- Content: Weekly blog highlights (snippets)
- CTA: Read on blog
- Frequency: Weekly

### Sequence 4: Customer Retention (Ongoing)
**Trigger:** Active customer
**Goal:** Maximize adoption, retention, upsell/expand

**Email 1: Monthly Newsletter**
- Subject: "[Company] newsletter: [Month overview]"
- Content: New features, tips, customer stories
- CTA: Learn more on dashboard
- Frequency: Monthly

**Email 2: Upcoming Feature**
- Subject: "Coming soon: AI-powered recommendations"
- Content: Feature preview, benefits, launch date
- CTA: Early access signup
- Frequency: As needed (2-3x/month)

**Email 3: Webinar/Training**
- Subject: "[Name], join us for advanced training"
- Content: Training topic, date/time, agenda
- CTA: Register
- Frequency: Monthly

**Email 4: Win-Back (For At-Risk)**
- Subject: "We miss you! Here's what's new"
- Content: New features, special offer, roadmap
- CTA: Reactivate account
- Personalization: Usage analysis

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
✅ **Good:**
- "You're about to save $500k"
- "This 1 metric predicts employee turnover"
- "[Name], here's what we found"

❌ **Avoid:**
- "Check this out!"
- "Important announcement"
- "Open immediately!"

### From Line
- **Prospects:** From company name (brand building)
- **Trial/Customers:** From person's name (relationship building)

### Preview Text (Subheader)
- Unique message (different from subject)
- 40-50 characters
- Creates curiosity or benefit

### Content Length
- Mobile-first design (40% opens on mobile)
- Short sections (3-5 sentences max)
- Lots of white space
- 1 clear CTA (button, not text link)

### Personalization
- First name in greeting
- Company/industry reference
- Previous behavior trigger
- Dynamic content blocks

## A/B Testing

### Test 1: Subject Line
- **Control:** "How to reduce turnover by 25%"
- **Variant:** "You're losing $500k to turnover"
- **Test:** Send to 1,000 each, winner to remaining 8,000

### Test 2: Send Time
- **Option A:** Tuesday 9am (traditional)
- **Option B:** Thursday 3pm (afternoon)
- **Analysis:** Compare open rates by segment

### Test 3: CTA Button
- **Control:** Blue "Learn More" button
- **Variant:** Red "Start Free Trial" button
- **Analysis:** Click rate, conversion

### Test 4: Email Length
- **Control:** Long-form (1,000+ words)
- **Variant:** Short-form (250-500 words)
- **Analysis:** Open rate, read time, conversion

## Email List Hygiene

### Unsubscribe Management
- **1-click unsubscribe** (GDPR requirement)
- **Frequency preference** (option to reduce, not unsubscribe)
- **Preference center** (choose content types)
- **Re-engagement campaign** (inactive 3 months)

### Bounce Management
- **Hard bounce:** Remove immediately
- **Soft bounce:** Retry 3 times, then remove
- **Invalid email:** Flag for manual review

### Compliance
- **GDPR:** Consent on signup, right to delete
- **CCPA:** Privacy policy, opt-out option
- **CAN-SPAM:** Unsubscribe link in all emails
