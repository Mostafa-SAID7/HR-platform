# Content Marketing Strategy

Strategic content plan, editorial calendar, and content distribution across all channels.

---

## Content Strategy Overview

**Mission:** Establish thought leadership, educate market, drive leads, build community.

**Goals (Year 1):**
- 52 blog posts published (1/week)
- 100k organic visitors to website
- 10k email subscribers
- 50 content pieces (guides, whitepapers, templates)
- 1,000+ backlinks
- Top 3 ranking for 50+ keywords

**Budget:** $250k/year

---

## Content Types & Formats

### 1. Blog Posts (52/year)
**Format:** 1,500-2,500 words
**Topics:** How-to, trends, case studies, data insights
**Frequency:** 1/week (Tuesday 8am)
**Distribution:** Website, email, social, LinkedIn

**Process:**
- Research & outline: 2 hours
- Write first draft: 4 hours
- Edit & optimize: 2 hours
- Design/formatting: 1 hour
- Promotion: 1 hour
- Total: 10 hours

### 2. Whitepapers (4/year)
**Format:** 10-15 pages, gated content
**Topics:** Industry trends, benchmarks, best practices
**Frequency:** Quarterly
**Distribution:** Email campaigns, sales

### 3. Guides & Resources (12/year)
**Format:** 5-10 pages, interactive tools
**Topics:** How-to guides, checklists, templates
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Distribution:** Website, gated for leads

### 4. Case Studies (12/year)
**Format:** 2-3 pages, PDF + web
**Topics:** Customer success stories
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Distribution:** Website, sales, webinars

### 5. Infographics (12/year)
**Format:** Visual data representations
**Topics:** Statistics, processes, comparisons
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Distribution:** Social, blog, presentations

### 6. Videos (26/year)
**Format:** 2-5 minutes
**Types:** Demos, tutorials, testimonials, insights
**Frequency:** 2x/week
**Distribution:** YouTube, blog, social, email

### 7. Podcasts (12/year)
**Format:** 30-45 minutes
**Topics:** Industry interviews, trends, insights
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Distribution:** Spotify, Apple Podcasts, website

### 8. Webinars (12/year)
**Format:** 45-60 minutes, interactive
**Topics:** Deep dives into use cases
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Distribution:** LinkedIn, email, website

### 9. Email Newsletters (52/year)
**Format:** Weekly digest
**Topics:** Blog highlights, tips, news
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Distribution:** Email list

### 10. Social Content (260+/year)
**Format:** Posts, stories, videos
**Topics:** All of above, bite-sized
**Frequency:** Multiple daily
**Distribution:** All social platforms

## SEO Content Strategy

### Keyword Research (Top 50 Keywords)

**High Volume:**
- HR analytics tool (monthly searches: 8,900)
- People analytics (6,600)
- Employee turnover prediction (4,400)
- HR software (90,500)

**Mid Volume:**
- Turnover prediction (2,400)
- Hire success prediction (1,200)
- Pay equity analysis (880)
- Employee engagement tools (5,600)

**Long-Tail:**
- Best HR analytics for mid-market (320)
- How to predict employee turnover (480)
- Reduce employee turnover with data (210)

### Content Pillars
1. **Turnover & Retention** (20% of content)
2. **Hiring & Recruitment** (20% of content)
3. **Compensation & Equity** (15% of content)
4. **Engagement & Culture** (15% of content)
5. **Performance Management** (10% of content)
6. **HR Best Practices** (20% of content)

### On-Page SEO Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (< 60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description (120-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 tag with primary keyword
- [ ] 2-3 H2 tags with related keywords
- [ ] Internal links (3-5)
- [ ] External links (2-3)
- [ ] Image alt text (keyword-rich)
- [ ] Mobile optimized
- [ ] < 3 second load time
- [ ] Readability score 60+

### Link Building Strategy
- **Internal linking:** Every blog post links to 3-5 other posts
- **Guest posts:** 1 guest post per month on HR publications
- **Partnerships:** Co-marketing content with partners
- **PR:** Press releases generate media backlinks
- **Resource page:** Become a hub for HR data/resources

## Content Distribution Strategy

### Blog Posts
- Post on website (Tuesday 8am)
- Share on LinkedIn (immediately, 3x that week)
- Share on Twitter (3x first day, 2x following days)
- Add to email newsletter
- Share in Slack/community
- Repurpose into social snippets (10 pieces from 1 post)

### Webinars
- Promote 2 weeks before (email + social)
- 1 week before: Reminder + LinkedIn Event
- Day before: Final reminder (email + social)
- Day of: Live Twitter commentary
- Post-webinar: Email recording link
- Repurpose: Extract clips for social/YouTube

### Case Studies
- Publish on website
- Share with sales team
- Pitch to media
- Promote via email
- Share on LinkedIn (testimonial post)
- Use in ads (social proof)

### Guides & Resources
- Gate on website (lead magnet)
- Email to list
- Promote via ads
- Share in communities
- Use for webinar follow-up

### Videos
- Publish on YouTube
- Embed in blog posts
- Share on social media
- Include in emails
- Add transcripts for accessibility
