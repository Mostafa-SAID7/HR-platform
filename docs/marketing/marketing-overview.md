# Marketing Overview & Strategy

Comprehensive marketing strategy, goals, positioning, and execution plan for HR Analytics Platform.

---

## Executive Summary

**Mission:** Transform mid-market HR with intelligent, real-time people analytics powered by AI.

**Year 1 Goal:** Achieve 15% brand awareness, generate 500 MQLs/month, reach $20M pipeline.

**Budget:** $1M for 2026

---

## Market Opportunity

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $63.75B
- Mid-market (250-2,000 employees): 125,000 companies
- Average spend on HR tech: $500k/year
- TAM: 125,000 × $500k = $62.5B

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $5B
- Focus on HR Analytics specifically
- 10,000 companies × $500k = $5B

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $150-200M (Year 1-3)
- 300-400 customers × $60k average ACV = $18-24M revenue
- 5% market share in focused segment

---

## Positioning Statement

**Claim:** The modern, AI-powered HR analytics platform built for mid-market companies.

**Target:** HR leaders at companies with 250-2,000 employees who want data-driven people decisions.

**Differentiation:** Real-time insights, mobile-first, affordable, easy to implement (days not months).

**Why It Matters:** Reduce turnover by 25%, hire 40% faster, save $2-5M annually.

## Risk & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Long Sales Cycle
**Impact:** Leads generated won't convert until Q3-Q4
**Mitigation:** 
- Start lead gen immediately
- Nurture with email sequence
- Track pipeline carefully

### Risk 2: Competitive Pressure
**Impact:** Budget required for paid ads increases
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on organic (SEO, content)
- Build brand (reduce ad dependency)
- Emphasize differentiation

### Risk 3: Team Scaling
**Impact:** Can't execute at 10x marketing level
**Mitigation:**
- Hire early (Q1)
- Use agencies for overflow
- Focus on high-leverage activities

### Risk 4: Market Education
**Impact:** Prospects don't understand value
**Mitigation:**
- Content marketing (educate early)
- Customer testimonials (proof)
- Webinars & events (direct engagement)
